ABOUT US

HOURS: 7am-4pm

Calico Coffee is a specialty coffee shop operating out of a remodeled schoolhouse. We work with local and national roasters to bring a variety of coffees to our menu. We collaborate with local bakers, retailers, vendors, and farms to bring quality products to our customers.
